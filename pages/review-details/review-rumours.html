Rumours

Fleetwood Mac

It’s almost impossible to separate the music from the soap opera, but if you strip away the lore, Rumours is a masterclass in rhythm section tension. Mick Fleetwood and John McVie are the only reason this thing doesn't float away into soft-rock oblivion; they provide a dry, punchy skepticism to Buckingham and Nicks’ fairy-tale melodies. The production is so clean it feels pharmaceutical, making this record a perfect pop object.

Released: February 4, 1977.

Contributed: Mick Fleetwood (drums),
John McVie (bass), Christine McVie (keys/vocals), Stevie Nicks(vocals),
Ken Caillat (engineer) and Lindsey Buckingham (guitar/vocals).
